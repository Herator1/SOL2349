
## ⚡ Reflex Booster

**Cat:** Combat  
**Type:** Neural  
**mSpace:** 3

A high-speed neural acceleration system that allows explosive bursts of action under pressure.

### Reflex Spike

**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +2

Immediately gain **1 additional Action**.

This Action may be used:

- on your turn
- or as a **Reactive Action** if a valid trigger exists

This extra Action cannot be used to activate **Reflex Spike** again.

### Neural Correction

**Type:** Reactive  
**Trigger:** after you fail a combat roll  
**Load:** +3  
**Limit:** 1× per round

Reroll the failed combat roll. You must use the new result.

## 🎯 Targeting Suite

**Cat:** Combat  
**Type:** Neural / Optic  
**mSpace:** 2

A predictive combat targeting package built to turn one firing window into a decisive hit.

### Predictive Targeting

**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +2

Your **next ranged attack this turn** gains all of the following:

- **Tactical Edge**
- ignore movement penalties
- reduce the target’s cover bonus by **2**

This affects only one attack.

### Recoil Dampening

**Type:** Passive

When making a second ranged attack in the same round, ignore the first repetition penalty.

### Lock-On

**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +2

Choose one visible target. Until the start of your next turn, your attacks against that target ignore movement penalties.

## 💥 Breach Assault Node

**Cat:** Combat  
**Type:** Neural / Muscular  
**mSpace:** 3

A violent close-assault combat package built for shock entry, force, and target break.

### Shock Entry

**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +2

Move up to **5 m** toward a visible target and immediately make **1 melee or shotgun attack**.

This attack gains **Tactical Edge**.

This movement does not ignore walls, sealed barriers, or major obstacles, but it ignores ordinary hesitation from tight terrain, clutter, or unstable footing.

### Violent Momentum

**Type:** Passive

If you reduce a target to **0 Guard** with a melee or shotgun attack, your **next melee or shotgun attack this turn** ignores the first repetition penalty.

### Breach Response

**Type:** Reactive  
**Trigger:** a target enters your room, corridor section, doorway, or immediate reach  
**Load:** +2

Make **1 immediate melee or shotgun attack** against that target.

This counts as a **Reactive Action**.

## 🔪 Close Quarters Suite

**Cat:** Combat + Physical  
**Type:** Muscular / Neural  
**mSpace:** 3

A close-quarters assault implant built for rapid engagement, violent entry, and decisive melee pressure.

### Bull Rush

**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +2  
**Limit:** 1× per turn

Move up to **10 m** directly toward a visible target.

This movement ignores ordinary terrain penalties and minor movement hindrance.

### Impact Strike

**Type:** Follow-up  
**Load:** +1

Immediately after **Bull Rush**, make **1 melee attack** with **Tactical Edge**.

This attack counts normally toward repetition.

### Relentless Assault

**Type:** Passive

When making a second melee attack in the same round, ignore the first repetition penalty.

### Augmented Throw

**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1

Throw a melee weapon using **Melee** instead of **Firearms**.

The attack ignores ordinary short-range penalties.
