## 🧬 Nanomedical Core (Advanced)

**Cat:** Medicine  
**Type:** Nano / Internal  
**mSpace:** 3

An advanced autonomous medical system built for high-end emergency tissue stabilization and survival under critical conditions.

### Emergency Reconstruction
**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +2  
**Limit:** 1× per Extended Rest

Restore **2d6 Health** to yourself or a target in touch range.

This cannot be used on a target above **half Health**.

### Critical Stabilization
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1

A dying target in touch range is immediately **stabilized**.

This stops the death countdown, but does not remove Wounds.

### Nanomedical Repair
**Type:** Extended Use  
**Load:** +2  
**Limit:** 1× per Extended Rest

With proper conditions, tools, and time, begin treatment on **1 Wound**.

This does **not** automatically remove the Wound immediately. Instead, it allows that Wound to be treated during proper recovery without requiring a full external facility.

### Medical Scan
**Type:** Passive

You may immediately assess:
- major trauma
- internal instability
- bleeding risk
- critical condition

This gives useful medical information without requiring guesswork.

**Role:** high-end field medic

## 🩹 Field Medical System

**Cat:** Medicine  
**Type:** Bio / Support  
**mSpace:** 2

A compact battlefield treatment system built for stabilization, fast intervention, and short-term function under pressure.

### Rapid Treatment
**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +2  
**Limit:** 1× per target per Short Rest

Restore **1d6 Health** to yourself or a target in touch range.

A target cannot benefit from **Rapid Treatment** again until they complete a **Short Rest**.

### Stabilize Patient
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1

Stabilize a dying target in touch range.

If successful, the target stops dying and may act normally on their next turn if nothing else prevents it.

### Trauma Support
**Type:** Passive

When performing **Stabilize** or **First Aid**, gain **Tactical Edge**.

**Role:** reliable combat medic

## 🧠 Medical Assistant AI

**Cat:** Medicine  
**Type:** Neural / Support  
**mSpace:** 1

A predictive medical support intelligence that improves diagnosis, sequencing, and treatment reliability.

### Cognitive Assistance
**Type:** Passive

Gain **Tactical Edge** on:
- Trauma
- Surgery
- Cybernetics

### Procedure Optimization
**Type:** Passive  
**Limit:** 1× per round

When activating a **medical implant ability**, reduce its **Load cost by 1** (minimum **1**).

### Triage Prediction
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1

Choose one patient in reach or line of sight.

Until the end of your next turn:
- the next time that target would take **Health damage**, reduce it by **1d6**
- or the next **Stabilize** action performed on that target gains **Tactical Edge**

**Role:** medical specialist support

## 💉 Combat Stim Dispenser *(Medicine + Physical)*

**Cat:** Medicine + Physical  
**Type:** Injector / Mechanical  
**mSpace:** 2

An integrated injector system built to deliver fast emergency chemical support at close range.

### Stim Injection
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** touch  
**Load:** +2  
**Limit:** 1× per target per Short Rest

Choose one target in reach and apply one of the following until the start of your next turn:

- **Pain Suppression** — ignore penalties from **1 Wound**
- **Mobility Surge** — gain **+5 m movement**
- **Assault Boost** — gain **Tactical Edge** on the next physical action

A target may only benefit from **one active injected stim** at a time.

### Emergency Boost
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** touch  
**Load:** +3  
**Limit:** 1× per target per Short Rest

One target in reach gains **+1 Action** this turn.

Afterward, that target gains **+1 Mental Condition**.

**Role:** close-support stim specialist

## 🌿 Adaptive Recovery System *(Medicine + Survival)*

**Cat:** Medicine + Survival  
**Type:** Bio / Regulation  
**mSpace:** 2

A biological recovery regulator that helps the body remain functional and recover faster under sustained pressure.

### Recovery Optimization
**Type:** Passive

During a valid **Short Rest**:
- recover **+1 additional Health**
- reduce **Mental Condition by 1 additional level**, once per rest

### Endurance Support
**Type:** Passive

Gain **Tactical Edge** when resisting:
- fatigue
- environmental exhaustion
- recovery disruption

### Overload Buffer
**Type:** Passive

When making an **Overload** roll, roll **2d6** and keep the **lower** result.

**Role:** sustain / recovery specialist
