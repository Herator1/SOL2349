## 🦿 Kinetic Leg System

**Cat:** Physical  
**Type:** Muscular / Kinetic  
**mSpace:** 2

A lower-body enhancement system built for explosive movement, vertical repositioning, and hard impact.

### Burst Movement
**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +1

Move up to **10 m** immediately.

This movement:
- ignores ordinary terrain penalties
- may include jumps, climbs, or short vertical repositioning
- may be used on your turn or as part of movement if a rule allows it

### Enhanced Mobility
**Type:** Passive

Your jump distance is **doubled**.  
You treat falls as **one category less severe** where the GM uses fall severity.

### Impact Kick
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1

Make **1 melee attack**.

On a hit, choose one:
- push the target up to **3 m**
- knock the target **prone**, if the target is of similar size or unstable

**Role:** mobility / reposition

## 🦾 Titan Frame

**Cat:** Physical  
**Type:** Muscular / Reinforcement  
**mSpace:** 3

A reinforced upper-body frame built for force, control, and overpowering physical interaction.

### Brute Force
**Type:** Passive

Gain **Tactical Edge** on actions involving raw force, such as:
- forcing doors
- lifting heavy objects
- holding position against pressure
- breaking restraints
- overpowering physical resistance

### Power Strike

**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1

Make **1 melee attack**.

On a hit, choose one:

- if the target still has **Guard**, deal **+1d6 damage**
- push the target up to **2 m**  
    in **microgravity** or **zero-g**, push the target up to **5 m** instead

### Lift & Throw
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +2

Lift and throw:
- a heavy object
- or a target you can physically control

The thrown object or target becomes a **ranged attack** that deals **2d6 damage** on a hit.

At GM discretion, the target may also be:
- knocked **prone**
- repositioned
- slammed into cover, a wall, or another target

**Role:** strength / control

## 🩰 Inertial Agility Suite

**Cat:** Physical + Survival  
**Type:** Neural / Balance  
**mSpace:** 2

A balance and movement-control system built for rapid repositioning, evasive survival, and precise body control.

### Slip Step  
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1  
  
Immediately move up to **5 m**.  
  
This movement:  
- ignores ordinary movement penalties  
- may break line of sight if terrain allows it  
- may be used in spaces where normal movement would be awkward or unstable

### Evasion Protocol
**Type:** Reactive  
**Trigger:** you are targeted by an attack  
**Load:** +2  
**Limit:** 1× per turn

Before the attack roll is made, move up to **3 m**.

Then apply one of the following:
- gain **+2 DC** against that attack
- or, if you reach **full cover**, the attack automatically misses

### Fluid Control
**Type:** Passive

Gain **Tactical Edge** on:
- balance
- acrobatics
- movement control
- avoiding unstable footing or awkward repositioning

**Role:** evasion / survival

## 🕷️ Vector Grip System  
  
**Cat:** Physical  
**Type:** Muscular / Adhesion  
**mSpace:** 2  
  
A full-body grip and stabilization system built for controlled movement on unstable surfaces, EVA hull work, and zero-g positioning under pressure.  
  
### Surface Grip  
**Type:** Passive  
  
You can anchor yourself to suitable surfaces using enhanced grip pads, magnetic clamps, or micro-adhesion systems, depending on your gear and environment.  
  
This allows you to:  
- hold position on steep or inverted surfaces  
- remain stable during recoil, drift, or sudden movement  
- work on hulls, walls, or structural frames without ordinary loss of footing  
  
This does not let you ignore sealed surfaces, extreme heat, loose debris, or surfaces with no viable grip.  
  
### Anchored Movement  
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1  
  
Move across a wall, ceiling, hull, or other traversable surface up to your normal movement without suffering ordinary instability penalties.  
  
This may be used in:  
- low gravity  
- microgravity  
- zero-g  
- unstable terrain  
- exterior EVA environments  
  
### Hard Lock  
**Type:** Reactive  
**Trigger:** you would be pushed, knocked back, drift away, or lose stable position  
**Load:** +1  
**Limit:** 1× per turn  
  
Immediately anchor yourself.  
  
Choose one:  
- ignore the forced movement  
- or remain fixed in place and gain **Tactical Edge** on the next action that depends on stability or controlled position  
  
**Role:** adhesion / EVA stability / positional control
