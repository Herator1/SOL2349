The Solar System of SOL 2349 is mapped, inhabited, exploited, and studied — but it is not fully understood.

Beneath the visible structure of habitats, corporations, trade routes, and political conflict lies a second layer of reality: lost histories, missing data, unexplained events, buried artifacts, and truths that were forgotten, hidden, or never fully understood in the first place. Some of these mysteries are relics of [[The AI War]] and [[The Departure]]. Others are older, stranger, or simply harder to explain. A few may be nothing more than rumor, misinterpretation, or opportunistic myth. Others may point to something real.

This section collects the unresolved questions, fragmentary evidence, forbidden subjects, and half-buried anomalies of the setting. It includes archaeological enigmas, suppressed knowledge, unexplained phenomena, strange relics, persistent rumors, and the darker edges of history where certainty begins to break down.

Not every mystery has an answer. Not every answer is public.  
And not everything buried in the Solar System wants to be found.
