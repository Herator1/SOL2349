This section covers the major organized actors of *SOL 2349*.

Power in the Solar System is fragmented. No single authority controls everything. Instead, political blocs, megacorporations, criminal networks, and ideological movements compete across overlapping jurisdictions, infrastructure systems, trade routes, and habitats. Some govern openly, some operate through contracts and dependency, and some thrive in the gaps between law and enforcement.

These pages describe who holds power, who challenges it, and who shapes daily life across the Solar System.
