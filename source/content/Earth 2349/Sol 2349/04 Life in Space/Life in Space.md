This section explores what everyday human life looks like in *SOL 2349*.

Beyond ships, stations, and corporations, life in the Solar System is shaped by habitat design, scarcity, distance, culture, work, medicine, language, and adaptation. These pages focus on how people live, move, eat, speak, heal, and build communities across a harsh and fragmented interplanetary civilization.
