**Population:** ~10 million

Carthage Drift is one of the roughest and most openly compromised cities on [[Venus]]—a semi-lawless trade settlement shaped by smuggling, syndicate influence, off-book manufacturing, and informal dock economies. Smaller and less polished than [[Eos Haven]], it lacks the latter’s commercial legitimacy and structured ambiguity. What Eos Haven disguises through paperwork and layered legality, Carthage Drift often displays more openly.

The city grew around secondary freight platforms, low-priority transfer infrastructure, and underregulated industrial margins that larger corporate systems found useful but not worth fully controlling. Over time, that made it a natural home for operators who needed access without scrutiny: smugglers, illicit brokers, shadow manufacturers, labor bosses, ship chandlers, salvage crews, and syndicate-linked transport networks.

Carthage Drift is not merely a criminal underside to Venusian trade. It is one of the places where the planet’s off-book economy becomes physically visible. Cargo moves through improvised docks, informal warehousing tiers, unregistered fabrication units, and maintenance sectors patched together out of old industrial infrastructure. Ownership is often unclear, jurisdiction is fluid, and entire districts operate through personal guarantees, coercive reputation, and local enforcement rather than formal law.

This gives the city a distinct social character. Carthage Drift is poorer, harsher, and less stable than the major cloud cities, but also more openly transactional. People come here to disappear into freight flows, to buy what cannot be bought legally, to move what cannot be registered, or to build things no licensed manufacturer is willing to acknowledge.

The **[[Venus Syndicates]]** are not hidden here. Their presence shapes labor, access, debt, violence, and commerce so directly that the distinction between criminal infrastructure and civic order is often meaningless. Carthage Drift continues to function not because it is governed well, but because too many useful things happen there for anyone to shut it down completely.

If [[Eos Haven]] is Venus’s compromised port city, then Carthage Drift is what compromise looks like after the mask comes off.
