**Helios Prime** is the main orbital headquarters of **[[Helios]]**, one of the most powerful resource and energy corporations in the inner solar system. Positioned in Earth orbit, it functions as both an executive command center and a major logistics hub linking [[Earth]], [[Luna]], [[Mercury]], and the industrial corridors of the inner system.

Its real significance lies in coordination. Helios Prime manages transport contracts, resource flows, energy distribution agreements, and the scheduling architecture that connects mining operations, transmission arrays, fuel networks, and refining platforms across multiple worlds. Much of Helios’ power comes not merely from owning resources, but from controlling how and when they move.

At the same time, Helios Prime is also one of the most prestigious corporate habitats in near-Earth space. Its upper residential and executive sectors are built to project stability, scale, and industrial wealth: controlled gravity environments, high-security board suites, panoramic Earth-facing residence decks, private docking access, luxury hospitality zones, and insulated administrative layers reserved for senior management, major clients, and politically important visitors.

Unlike the harder industrial style of many Helios-operated environments, Helios Prime presents the corporation in its most polished form. It is clean, spacious, and deliberately reassuring—an orbital environment designed to communicate that the systems of modern civilization are in competent hands. That image is not incidental. It is part of the station’s function.

This creates a sharp internal contrast. Beneath the executive shells and prestige sectors lies a dense operational core of traffic control, logistics oversight, contract administration, network coordination, and support infrastructure. Helios Prime is therefore both a symbol and a machine: a place where luxury exists directly above the systems that make that luxury possible.

If [[Aurora Finance Hub]] represents the refined abstraction of capital, then Helios Prime represents something more concrete: **wealth built on control of mass, energy, and movement at planetary scale**.
