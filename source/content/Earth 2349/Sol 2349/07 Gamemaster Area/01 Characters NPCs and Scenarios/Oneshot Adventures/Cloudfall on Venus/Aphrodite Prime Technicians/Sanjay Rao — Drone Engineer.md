Sanjay is strongest when he is **not physically present**.

- operates through drones instead of direct exposure

## 📖 Optional Background Lore

Sanjay Rao built his career in places where people could not always go.

Orbital maintenance platforms, external infrastructure frames, relay arrays — environments where distance, vacuum, or risk made direct human work inefficient or dangerous. He learned to operate through machines, to trust sensor data, and to think in distributed systems rather than single locations.

For him, presence is optional.

What matters is access.

On Aphrodite Prime, that mindset becomes an advantage. The city is dense, layered, and full of systems that can be reached without being physically touched — if you know how to look for them. Where others see barriers, Sanjay sees alternative paths.

Players stepping into Sanjay should focus on:

- staying out of unnecessary danger
- using drones to gather information first
- thinking in multiple positions at once
- supporting the team through access and flexibility

He is not the one opening the door.

He is the one who already knows what’s behind it.
