# 🧭 Elias Varga

![[ChatGPT Image 8. Apr. 2026, 13_49_26.png|450]]

**Origin:** Earth  
**Background:** Helios operations specialist with an intelligence brief  
**Role:** Systems, hacking, communication, pressure control

Elias joined the _Asterion_ officially as a communications and operations specialist for [[Helios]]. Unofficially, he was placed aboard to observe, report, and quietly keep an eye on anything unusual the mission might uncover.

He comes from a corporate environment on Earth where information mattered more than force. Before joining the ship, he worked in operations support, systems oversight, and internal security-adjacent roles — the kind of work where access logs, unusual behavior, and small inconsistencies mattered.

He is not a field soldier and not a classic spy thriller type. He is useful because he notices things early, understands systems quickly, and knows how to shape a tense situation without drawing much attention to himself.

**Good motivations to play:** stay in control through information, keep dangerous situations manageable, decide for yourself where loyalty ends, and make sure you are never the last person to understand what is happening.

## 🧩 Implants

### 🖧 System Interface Node

**Category:** Operations  
**Type:** Neural / Interface  
**Mod Space:** 2

A neural control implant that lets Elias interact with nearby systems as if already plugged in.

**Remote Interface (Passive)**  
You may interact with a connected or exposed system within **20 m** if you have line of sight, a valid access point, or a clear local interface path. This allows opening or closing doors, cycling locks, operating cameras, triggering panels, or activating simple mechanisms. It does **not** bypass security by itself.

**Fast Interface (Augmented, Load +1, 1× per round)**  
Perform one simple system interaction **without spending an Action**. This can be used on your turn or reactively if a valid trigger exists.

**Extended Control (Active, 1 Action, Load +2)**  
Take control of one simple connected system within range until the start of your next turn: a door, camera, lift control, local mechanism, or lightly secured turret or sensor.

### 📣 Psychological Warfare Suite

**Category:** Psychology + Operations  
**Type:** Neural / Broadcast  
**Mod Space:** 3

A pressure-and-control suite designed to spread confusion, strain, and tactical disadvantage.

**Disruption Field (Active, 1 Action, Range 10 m, Load +2)**  
Choose all targets in a small visible zone within range. They suffer **Setback** on their next action.

**Coordinated Pressure (Active, 1 Action, Range 10 m, Load +2)**  
Choose one visible target or small visible group. Until the start of your next turn, the first ally to act against each affected target gains **Tactical Edge**.

**Narrative Control (Passive)**  
Gain **Tactical Edge** on persuasion under pressure, deception in unstable situations, and crowd influence where fear, confusion, or uncertainty are already present.

## 🧭 Play Elias Like This

Elias is strongest when he controls information rather than bodies.

He is good at:

- operating systems remotely
    
- opening, closing, locking, and rerouting access
    
- spotting what other people miss
    
- shaping tense scenes through pressure and coordination
    
- supporting allies without standing in the middle of the fight
    

He is weaker in:

- direct combat
    
- taking hits
    
- brute-force problem solving
    
- situations where subtlety is no longer possible
    

At the table, play him as observant, composed, and careful with words. He usually knows more than he says. He is not trying to dominate a room by charisma; he is trying to understand it fast enough to shape what happens next.
