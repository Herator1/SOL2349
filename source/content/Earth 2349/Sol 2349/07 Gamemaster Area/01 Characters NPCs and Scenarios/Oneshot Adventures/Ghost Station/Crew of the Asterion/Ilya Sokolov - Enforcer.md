# 🧭 Ilya Sokolov

![[Sokolov.png|450]]

**Origin:** Venus  
**Background:** Former lower-tier enforcer, now security muscle on the _Asterion_  
**Role:** Frontline, protection, intimidation

Ilya grew up in the lower tiers of Venus, where local power, debt, and violence often mattered more than formal rules. He worked for years as an enforcer and protection man in environments where people settled problems through pressure, reputation, and short bursts of force.

He is not a thug and not a berserker. He is controlled, efficient, and used to stepping in when a situation turns physical.

At some point he left that life behind, likely through Kaito or a similar contact, and ended up on the _Asterion_ doing legal work for [[Helios]]. He is still the crew member you want closest when things go bad. He is there to keep threats contained, protect the others, and end violent problems quickly.

**Good motivations to play:** protect the crew, keep control when things turn dangerous, avoid being used again, and prove that violence works best when applied carefully.

## 🧩 Implants

### ⚡ Reflex Booster

**Category:** Combat  
**Type:** Neural  
**Mod Space:** 3

A high-speed neural acceleration system built for explosive action under pressure.

**Reflex Spike (Augmented, Load +2)**  
Gain **1 additional Action** immediately. It can be used on your turn or as a Reactive Action if a valid trigger exists. It cannot be used to activate **Reflex Spike** again.

**Neural Correction (Reactive, Load +3, 1× per round)**  
After you fail a combat roll, reroll it. You must use the new result.

---

### 💪 Titan Frame

**Category:** Physical  
**Type:** Muscular / Reinforcement  
**Mod Space:** 3

A reinforced frame built for force, control, and overpowering physical resistance.

**Brute Force (Passive)**  
Gain **Tactical Edge** on actions involving raw force: forcing doors, lifting heavy objects, holding position, breaking restraints, or overpowering resistance.

**Power Strike (Active, 1 Action, Load +1)**  
Make 1 melee attack. On a hit, choose one: deal **+1d6 damage** if the target still has Guard, push the target up to **2 m**, or push up to **5 m** in microgravity or zero-g.

**Lift & Throw (Active, 1 Action, Load +2)**  
Lift and throw a heavy object or a physically controlled target. The thrown object or target becomes a ranged attack dealing **2d6 damage** on a hit. At GM discretion, the target may also be knocked prone, repositioned, or slammed into cover, a wall, or another target.

## 🧭 Play Ilya Like This

Ilya is built for direct action. He is strongest when he can seize tempo, hold ground, and make other people back down.

He is good at:

- short violent engagements
    
- protecting choke points
    
- forcing enemies out of position
    
- taking punishment and staying functional
    
- ending hesitation with action
    

He is weaker in:

- delicate social situations
    
- diplomacy
    
- long technical problem-solving
    
- subtle manipulation
    

At the table, play him as quiet, observant, and physically calm. He does not posture much. He watches, judges, and acts decisively once someone crosses a line.
