# 🔧 Mateo Álvarez

![[Alvarez.png|450]]

**Origin:** Asteroid Belt  
**Background:** Belt-raised engineer from a mining family  
**Role:** Systems, repair, technical problem solving

Mateo grew up in the Belt in a mining family, where machinery, pressure integrity, and maintenance were everyday survival issues. He did not become an engineer through prestige or theory. He became one because on stations, platforms, and work hulls, somebody always has to know how to keep failing systems alive.

Before joining the _Asterion_, he worked on mining infrastructure, industrial maintenance, and contract engineering jobs where equipment was old, parts were missing, and repair windows were short. He is used to patching damaged systems under pressure and making imperfect hardware work long enough to matter.

He is not a talker and not a natural leader. He is the person who notices what is about to fail, keeps the ship functioning, and finds workable solutions when everyone else is already dealing with the consequences.

**Good motivations to play:** keep the crew alive through competence, prove you can fix what others would abandon, protect the systems that keep people breathing, and stay useful no matter how bad things get.

## 🧩 Implants

### 🛠️ Engineering Frame (Third Arm Rig)

**Category:** Engineering  
**Type:** Mechanical  
**Mod Space:** 3

A mounted auxiliary engineering arm built for fieldwork in tight, damaged, or awkward spaces.

**Integrated Tool Arm (Passive)**  
You have a functional third utility arm. It can hold or manipulate tools, operate panels or controls, perform simple engineering handling without using one of your main hands, and counts as always having access to an integrated Multi-Tool.

**Tool Override (Active, 1 Action, Load +1)**  
Immediately perform one engineering interaction using the integrated arm: cut, drill, force open, seal, brace, or manipulate a panel or housing. This works even if both main hands are occupied, as long as the arm can reach the target.

---

### 🌬️ Adaptive Life Support

**Category:** Survival  
**Type:** Bio / Regulatory  
**Mod Space:** 3

A survival-regulation implant built to keep the body functional through exposure and environmental stress.

**Environmental Shielding (Active, 1 Action, Load +1)**  
Until the start of your next turn, ignore one ordinary environmental penalty caused by heat, cold, toxic exposure, low oxygen, or background radiation. It does not protect against catastrophic exposure, direct vacuum damage, or extreme hazards by itself.

**Vacuum Tolerance (Reactive, Load +2)**  
When suddenly exposed to vacuum or severe atmosphere loss, remain functional for 2 turns without immediately suffering the full normal consequences. Movement is limited, speech and precision may be restricted, and it does not replace proper EVA gear.

**Survival Recovery (Passive)**  
During a valid Short Rest, recover **+1 additional Health** if the rest happens in a hostile, exposed, or resource-poor environment.

## 🧭 Play Mateo Like This

Mateo is strongest when systems are failing and other people are starting to panic.

He is good at:

- repair work under pressure
    
- damaged infrastructure
    
- awkward engineering access
    
- low-g and hazardous-environment problem solving
    
- improvising with bad tools, damaged parts, and limited time
    

He is weaker in:

- social scenes
    
- command
    
- direct combat
    
- bluffing or intimidation
    

At the table, play him as practical, focused, and technically minded. He responds to danger by fixing, sealing, rerouting, bracing, or improvising. He usually says less than he knows, and he tends to think in systems rather than personalities.
