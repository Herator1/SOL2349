## Three Ready-to-Drop Clue Moments

Use these three clues as fixed anchor points during play.  
They are not optional variants. They are the default information path of the scenario.

---

### 1. Dead Corridor Service Hatch

**Location:**  
In the dead corridor network between **Dock 1** and the **spin-transfer lift**, at a half-jammed manual service hatch.

**What is there:**  
A dark utility junction with a smeared handprint near the manual release wheel, a dropped cargo tether clip, and a still-functioning maintenance terminal with a cracked display.

**What the players can recover:**  
A short cached terminal fragment:

> **TRANSFER STATUS:** SUSPENDED  
> **HOLD ACCESS:** LOCKED  
> **AUTHORITY STATE:** DISPUTED  
> **CREW STATUS:** SPLIT  
> **NOTE:** Await confirmation before moving cargo.

**What this clue establishes:**  
- the conflict was internal, not just an outside attack  
- the station crisis centered on cargo access  
- command authority had already broken down before open violence

**GM use:**  
This is the first hard sign that the situation on Ghost Station was a rupture over something specific, not random station collapse.

---

### 2. Survivor Hide Pocket

**Location:**  
In a sealed off-axis maintenance recess just beside the **spin-transfer lift / neutral threshold**, hidden behind a partially removed inspection panel.

**What is there:**  
A cramped maintenance pocket containing one emergency blanket, two empty water pouches, a blood-stained sleeve patch from the freighter crew, and **Talia Serrano**, weak but conscious.

**What Talia says once the players get her stable enough to talk:**  

> “They kept saying it was just recovery. Property. Procedures.  
> But people were still here. People were still alive.  
> We were supposed to move it and leave. Then command broke, the crew split, and everything turned into a grab.”

**What this clue establishes:**  
- the station was still inhabited when the recovery operation began  
- the freighter crew was directly involved in moving the relic  
- the break into factions happened after an attempted transfer, not before

**GM use:**  
This is the humanizing clue. It reframes Ghost Station as a place with people inside it, not just a smuggler ruin or battlefield.

---

### 3. Freighter Hold Transfer Record

**Location:**  
At **Dock 2**, inside the freighter near the secured hold access, on a damaged cargo control panel beside the breached transfer frame.

**What is there:**  
A sealed cargo bay with impact damage, one broken containment bracket, two dead crew in old vac-suits, and a cargo control screen still running on emergency power.

**What the players can recover:**  
A final interrupted transfer record:

> **CARGO TRANSFER ABORTED**  
> **DESTINATION:** GHOST STATION INTERNAL HOLD  
> **EXTERNAL BUYER CONFIRMATION:** PENDING  
> **COMMAND OVERRIDE:** CONTESTED  
> **SECURED ITEM REMAINS IN HOLD**  
> **LOG END: manual interruption / weapons discharge detected**

**What this clue establishes:**  
- the relic was physically present and actively being transferred  
- there was an intended outside buyer  
- the mutiny and violence began during the transfer dispute itself

**GM use:**  
This is the confirming clue. From this point on, the players should understand that the relic is real, central, and the reason the station broke apart.
