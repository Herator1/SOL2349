## Implants

### Systems Interface Node

**Category:** Operations  
**mSpace:** 2

**Passive — Remote Interface**  
You may interact with a connected or exposed system within **20 m** if you have line of sight, a valid access point, or a clear local interface path. This allows things like opening or closing doors, cycling locks, operating cameras, and activating simple mechanisms. It does **not** bypass security by itself.

**Augmented — Fast Interface**  
**Load:** +1  
**Limit:** 1× per round  
Perform one simple system interaction **without spending an Action**. This can be used on your turn or reactively if a valid trigger exists.

---

### Intrusion Suite

**Category:** Operations  
**mSpace:** 3

**Augmented — Quick Breach**  
**Range:** 10 m  
**Load:** +2  
Immediately attempt to access or bypass one protected system within range, without needing extended setup.

**Active — Data Extraction**  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Load:** +1  
If you already have access to a system, immediately pull one useful package of data, such as logs, identities, routes, camera records, access history, or stored instructions.

**Passive — Ghost Access**  
When you successfully bypass a system, you may leave no obvious sign of intrusion unless the system is actively monitored or exceptionally secure.

---

Elias uses all **5 Mod Space** exactly: starting Mod Space is 5, implants use Mod Space, and this is a hard capacity limit unless another rule increases it.

## Genemods

### Social Conditioning

Gain Tactical Edge when issuing instructions, establishing order, or exerting formal authority.  
**Drawback:** Deception and improvisational identity-play feel unnatural; suffer Setback on Deception.

### Neural Acceleration

Always act as if mentally prepared for sudden change; gain Tactical Edge on reaction-based perception or timing rulings.  
**Drawback:** Mental shock hits harder; when you gain a Mental Condition from overload or trauma, the GM may escalate the reaction more sharply.

Genemods are always active, require no Actions, generate no Load, do not use Mod Space, and characters may have up to 2 at character creation.

## Purpose in the Scenario

Elias is the team member who:

- reads **access history, logs, routes, and camera records**
- controls or exploits **doors, panels, cameras, and local systems**
- supports **private confrontations** by staying calm under pressure
- keeps the audit procedural when the station starts pushing back
