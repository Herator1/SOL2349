## Implants

### Empathic Insight Module

**Category:** Psychology  
**mSpace:** 2

**Emotional Read — Passive**  
Quickly assess a target’s emotional state, likely intent, visible instability, and likely pressure points when enough cues are present.

**Insight Focus — Passive**  
Gain Tactical Edge on **Insight** and on reading deception, instability, or emotional weakness.

**Influence Window — Active**  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 10 m  
**Load:** +1  
Choose one visible target within range. Your next social interaction against that target gains Tactical Edge.

---

### Persuasion Suite

**Category:** Social  
**mSpace:** 2

**Leverage Point — Passive**  
Quickly identify one likely emotional or practical leverage point in a conversation, such as fear, desire, status concern, insecurity, urgency, or personal motive. The GM should provide one usable angle when enough information is present.

**Directed Argument — Active**  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 10 m  
**Load:** +1  
Choose one visible target within range. Your next social interaction against that target gains Tactical Edge.

**Pressure Shift — Augmented**  
**Load:** +2  
Choose one visible target within range. Until the start of your next turn, that target suffers Setback on attempts to resist persuasion, negotiation, or command pressure from you.

---

### Stress Regulation System

**Category:** Psychology  
**mSpace:** 2

**Mental Stability — Passive**  
Gain Tactical Edge on **Stress Control** and other rolls made to resist mental collapse, panic, or overload-related breakdown.

**Stabilize Mind — Active**  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** touch  
**Load:** +1  
**Limit:** 1× per target per Short Rest  
Reduce the target’s Mental Condition by 1 level.

**Override Panic — Augmented**  
**Load:** +2  
Ignore penalties from your current Mental Condition until the start of your next turn. This does not remove the condition itself.

## Mod Space Note

Those three implants would total **6 mSpace**, so they do **not** fit a starting character with Mod Space 5 unless you buy additional Mod Space during character creation. Starting Mod Space is 5, and increasing base stats uses the same advancement logic as attributes.

So for a strict starting build, Samira should take **either**:

### Option A — More socially aggressive

- Empathic Insight Module (2)
- Persuasion Suite (2)
- leave 1 mSpace unused

### Option B — More stable investigator

- Empathic Insight Module (2)
- Stress Regulation System (2)
- leave 1 mSpace unused

I recommend **Option B** for this scenario, because it keeps her strong in interviews and much better during long, high-pressure confrontations. The Empathic Insight Module and Stress Regulation System are both on the Psychology Implants page.
