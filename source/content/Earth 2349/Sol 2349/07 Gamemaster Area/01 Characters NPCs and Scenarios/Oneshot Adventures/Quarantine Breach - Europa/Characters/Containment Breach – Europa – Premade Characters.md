## Team Structure

**Dr. Mira Aniksen**  
Biological Systems Auditor  
Focus on xenobiology, biosafety, laboratory workflows, sample logic, and contamination risk.

**Inspector Elias Ward**  
Systems Compliance Officer  
Focus on procedural control, security protocols, movement logs, access records, and formal investigation.

**Eng. Rian Ortega**  
Engineering and Robotics Specialist  
Focus on drones, maintenance systems, hardware use, transfer mechanisms, and physical material flow.

**Samira Voss**  
Liaison and Behavioral Analyst  
Focus on interviews, crew interaction, institutional pressure, deception, and social investigation.

## Purpose

The team is built to function inside a highly controlled technical environment.

**Dr. Mira Aniksen** covers the biological and scientific side of the investigation.  
**Inspector Elias Ward** covers systems, procedure, and authority.  
**Eng. Rian Ortega** covers engineering logic, robotics, and hardware.  
**Samira Voss** covers interviews, trust, and social pressure.
