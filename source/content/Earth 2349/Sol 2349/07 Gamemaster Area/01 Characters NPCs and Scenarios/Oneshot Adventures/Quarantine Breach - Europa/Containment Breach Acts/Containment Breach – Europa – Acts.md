**Act 1 – Arrival**  
The players receive their briefing, travel from the outer station to Europa, descend below the ice, and enter the research system. This act establishes the setting, operational constraints, and the first internal contacts.

**Act 2 – Investigation**  
The inspection begins. The players review records, inspect facilities, speak to crew members, and follow technical and social leads. This act forms the sandbox core of the scenario.

**Act 3 – Confrontation**  
By this stage, the players should have identified one or more underlying causes of the discrepancies. The next transfer cycle creates pressure and forces a decision on how and when to intervene.

**Act 4 – Outcomes and Rewards**  
The players’ actions determine what is contained, what is exposed, and what remains unresolved. This section covers consequences, institutional responses, and possible rewards.
