## Overview

**Location:** Subsurface Ocean, Europa  
**Scenario Type:** Investigation / Closed Environment  
**Playtime:** One-Shot (4–6 hours)

---

## Premise

The Jovian League Scientific Consortium has initiated a routine audit of a subsurface research operation beneath Europa’s ice.

Small discrepancies have been identified in the recorded handling of biological samples. The deviations are minor but outside expected tolerance.

An independent inspection team is deployed to review procedures, validate records, and confirm system integrity.

---

## Setting

A long-duration research sub operates beneath Europa’s ice, collecting and processing biological samples from the subsurface ocean.

The system is tightly controlled. All biological material must remain on Europa. Only data, personnel, and certified equipment may leave the environment.

The sub operates continuously with periodic supply and transfer runs to and from a surface-linked station.

The environment is stable, procedural, and isolated.

---

## Player Role

The players are part of the inspection team.

They have access to:

- operational data
- laboratory areas
- crew interviews

They do not take over command of the station.

The crew continues normal operations during the inspection.

---

## Structure

The scenario is divided into three acts:

**Arrival** – Transfer to Europa, entry into the station, initial orientation.  
**Investigation** – Review of processes, data, and personnel.  
**Resolution** – Evaluation of findings and decision on further action.

---

## Tone

The scenario focuses on:

- controlled environments
- procedural systems
- small inconsistencies
- gradual escalation

There is no immediate crisis at the start.
