This section contains ready-to-play characters created for the one-shot scenarios of **SOL 2349**. These premades are designed with their **original scenarios in mind**. Each one fits into a specific situation, team structure, and narrative context — with defined roles, internal dynamics, and built-in tension.

They can absolutely be reused in other sessions. But they work best when treated as part of a larger setup, not as isolated builds.

These are **not optimized characters** in the traditional sense. They are not built to maximize efficiency or combat performance. They are built for **consistency, role clarity, and group dynamics**.

Expect:

- strengths that matter in context
- gaps that create tension
- overlapping roles and friction within the team

Most characters are built at around **50 PP**, representing **experienced professional teams** — for example in scenarios like _Operation Kestrel Landing_. Some go beyond that, depending on the operational level and narrative needs of the scenario.

When selecting characters, keep this in mind:

- power level varies between scenarios
- teams are intentionally asymmetrical
- not every character is meant to shine in every situation

These characters are entry points into a situation — not balanced endpoints. Take them as they are, step into their roles, and let the situation do the rest.
