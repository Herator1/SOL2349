## 1. Overview

**Acheron Defense Systems (ADS)** is the most prominent arms manufacturer and private security provider in the Solar System. While many megacorporations maintain internal security divisions, Acheron specializes entirely in the development and deployment of **military-grade technologies and contracted combat forces**.

The company’s headquarters are located in **Aphrodite Prime**, a large orbital corporate city above Venus that serves as a financial and industrial hub for several powerful corporations. From there, Acheron manages a network of weapons laboratories, orbital shipyards, and training facilities.

Acheron’s internal structure resembles a hybrid of corporate hierarchy and organized syndicate. Individual divisions control specific markets—such as orbital defense systems or ground combat hardware—and often operate with considerable autonomy. This structure has made the corporation extremely flexible but has also contributed to its reputation for **mafia-like influence networks**, protection rackets, and aggressive contract enforcement.

The corporation frequently operates in legally ambiguous zones where megacorporations compete over mining rights, shipping routes, or infrastructure control. As a result, Acheron is both a **supplier of security and a frequent participant in corporate conflicts**.

## 2. Key Facilities

**Aphrodite Prime (Venus Orbit)** – Corporate headquarters, financial center, and main command hub for Acheron operations.

**Phobos Combat Testing Range (Mars)** – Large testing and training complex used for weapons trials and security force exercises.

**Vulcan Forge Shipyards** – Orbital manufacturing facilities specializing in missile systems, railgun components, and heavy combat platforms.

## 3. Example Products

- **AR-series assault rifles** designed for vacuum and low-gravity combat
- **Electromagnetic railguns** used on security ships and orbital platforms
- **Exo-combat suits** for enhanced mobility and survivability in hostile environments
- **Cruise missiles and torpedo systems** for anti-ship operations
- **Advanced targeting and fire-control systems** for spacecraft and automated defenses
Acheron also provides **contracted security forces**, often deployed to protect corporate installations, escort high-value cargo, or stabilize disputed territories.

## 4. Controversies and Conflicts

Acheron Defense Systems has repeatedly been accused of escalating corporate disputes by supplying weapons to multiple parties in the same conflict. Several investigations have linked the company to **protection rackets within major orbital ports**, where shipping companies allegedly pay “security fees” to avoid piracy or sabotage.

The corporation’s private security forces have also been involved in numerous violent incidents, particularly in the Asteroid Belt, where Acheron units have clashed with transport collectives and independent mining operations. Critics argue that Acheron profits from instability, while the company maintains that it merely provides **necessary security in an inherently dangerous frontier economy**.
