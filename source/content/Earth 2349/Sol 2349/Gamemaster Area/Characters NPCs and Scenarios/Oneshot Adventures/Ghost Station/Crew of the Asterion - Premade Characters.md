## 🚀 The Crew of the _Asterion_

This crew is a **ready-to-play starter group** for the _Ghost Station_ scenario in **SOL 2349**. They are the minimal working crew of the _Asterion_, a small [[Helios]] survey vessel operating in the [[Asteroid Belt]]. They are freelancers, contractors, and specialists — not heroes, soldiers, or chosen protagonists.

Their job is simple: survey rock, assess value, log data, and move on.

Each character fills a clear role aboard the ship, covering the core needs of a small industrial Belt vessel: command, engineering, operations, medicine, and security. Together, they are designed to introduce the main themes and systems of the game:

- survival under pressure
- technical problem-solving
- tension between competing interests
- and the realities of working inside a fragmented, corporate-driven solar system

The _Asterion_ is not a warship, and this crew is not built for a fair fight. They are competent enough to survive, useful enough to matter, and vulnerable enough for the scenario to stay dangerous.

That is exactly what makes them a good entry crew for _Ghost Station_.
