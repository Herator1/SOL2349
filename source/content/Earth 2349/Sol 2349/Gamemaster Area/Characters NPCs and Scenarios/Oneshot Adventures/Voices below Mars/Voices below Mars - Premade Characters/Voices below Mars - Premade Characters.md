This section contains the premade characters for **Voices Below Mars**.

These characters were designed specifically for this scenario and together form the crew of the **MCRV Dustbreaker**: a believable working Mars salvage team with clear roles, practical skills, and strong fit for the scenario’s tone.

They are **not optimized for maximum mechanical power**. Instead, they are built for:

- team consistency
- workplace logic
- scenario usefulness
- flavour and role clarity

That makes them well suited for **Voices Below Mars**, where the focus is on salvage work, technical problem-solving, pressure, and difficult choices rather than heroic power fantasy.

They can also be used in other **SOL 2349** games if you want a ready-made Mars surface crew.

- [[Tomas Ibarra]] — Noctis Labyrinthus, Mars — Driver / Vehicle Operator
- [[Naila Farouk]] — Valles Marineris habitat chain, Mars — Mechanic / Salvage Engineer
- [[Caio Mendonça]] — Nova São Paulo, Earth — Operations Specialist
- [[Linh Trần]] — Tycho Industrial Complex, Luna — Medic / Life Support Specialist
