## Quick Use

If you need a fast scene, combine:

**hazard + location + cause + one person under pressure**

Example:

**sudden loss of atmosphere**  
in **a docking ring**  
caused by **orbital debris**  
with **a dock supervisor trying to stop panic**

That is already a playable scene.

## Severity Shortcut

If needed, judge the hazard like this:

- **minor** — dangerous but contained
- **serious** — immediate action needed
- **critical** — lives at risk right now
- **cascading** — failure will trigger more failures

## Best Causes for Fast Play

If you do not know why the hazard happened, default to one of these:

- bad maintenance
- rushed work
- hidden sabotage
- aging infrastructure
- impact damage
- overload
- bad data
- human error
