Use this page when the players recover, inspect, buy, steal, or stumble across something valuable.

This page separates two related but different things:

- **Salvage** — normal but valuable equipment, materials, and recoverable hardware
- **Relics** — unusual machine-age remains, fragments, or finds with unclear origin, value, or danger

Roll or choose freely.

---

# AI Relic Tables

Relics are not normal salvage.

They are usually stranger, rarer, harder to price, and harder to understand. Even when they appear inert, their value may lie in material properties, design logic, partial functionality, or the simple fact that somebody important badly wants them.

## Quick Salvage Formula

**[category] + [find] + [condition/value]**

Example:

**industrial grade**

- **refinery control systems**
- **stripped of one critical part, but valuable as a rare spare-parts source**

## Quick Relic Formula

**[category] + [find] + [condition/significance]**

Example:

**data**

- **black-box memory core**
- **encrypted, and valuable because it points to a larger missing system**

## Good Defaults for SOL 2349

When in doubt:

- normal salvage should be **useful**
- relics should be **unclear**
- the best finds should be valuable **and** troublesome

A good find should raise questions like:

- who wants this?
- who claims it?
- who can use it?
- who would rather it disappear?

## In One Sentence

Good salvage solves practical problems. Good relics create new ones.
