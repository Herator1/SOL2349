# Gamemaster Area

_SOL 2349_ is currently in alpha.

The system is intentionally flexible. Rules, equipment, encounter difficulty, and narrative pacing can and should be adjusted to fit your table, your players, and the tone of the scenario. Do not hesitate to simplify, improvise, or modify mechanics when it improves flow, tension, or clarity.

This section is intended for gamemasters. It collects the tools, background guidance, reference material, and scenario content needed to run _SOL 2349_ in a way that feels consistent with the setting and its intended tone.

⚠️ **Players: Do not read beyond this point.**
