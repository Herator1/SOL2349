## 🛰️ Ghost Station

_Ghost Station_ is a **self-contained one-shot scenario** set in the harsh, industrial reality of _SOL 2349_. It places the players in a damaged vessel, cut off from support, and forced to seek refuge in a location that was never meant to be found.

The adventure focuses on **tension, uncertainty, and decision-making under pressure**. It is not a combat-driven scenario, but conflict is always present — and often unavoidable. Resources are limited, information is incomplete, and every action has consequences that can shift the situation in unexpected ways.

The tone is grounded, slow-burning, and atmospheric. Inspired by hard science fiction, the scenario emphasizes **physical realism, environmental hazards, and human behavior in extreme conditions**. The station itself is not just a backdrop, but an active factor: unstable systems, failing infrastructure, and shifting conditions constantly influence what is possible.

At its core, _Ghost Station_ is about **interacting with a situation already in motion**. Multiple groups with conflicting goals occupy the same space, and none of them are in full control. The players are not there to follow a predefined path — they are there to navigate a fragile and escalating scenario.

The expected playtime is **3–5 hours**, depending on pacing, player decisions, and how quickly the situation escalates. The scenario is designed to be flexible: it can lean more into negotiation and problem-solving, or shift toward survival and confrontation if tensions rise.

There is no single correct outcome. Success is defined by what the players choose to prioritize — and what they are willing to risk to achieve it.
