## 🚀 The Crew of the _Asterion_

This crew represents a **ready-to-play starter group** for the _Ghost Station_ scenario in **SOL 2349**. They are a minimal Helios survey crew — freelancers, not heroes — sent into deep space to map and evaluate asteroid resources. Each character fills a clear role aboard the vessel, combining technical expertise, survival skills, and just enough combat capability to get into trouble.

These characters are designed for **new players** and introduce the core systems of the game:

- survival under pressure
- technical problem solving
- social tension and decision-making
- and the consequences of working in a corporate-controlled solar system

The _Asterion_ is not a military ship. The crew is not prepared for what they will encounter. That’s the point.
