This section collects short narrative pieces set across the world of **SOL 2349**.  
  
While much of the wiki focuses on technology, factions, habitats, and systems, these stories are meant to show what life in the Solar System actually feels like from the inside. They follow workers, scientists, crew members, settlers, travelers, and ordinary people as they move through a world shaped by distance, pressure, ambition, adaptation, and survival.  
  
For players, they offer a clearer sense of tone, atmosphere, and everyday reality. For GMs, they provide emotional reference points, social detail, and small glimpses into how the setting lives beyond pure lore.  
  
These are not grand histories. They are human-scale windows into life in space.
