## Solar System History (2030–2349)

### Current Era — 2349

The year is **2349**. Humanity has expanded across the entire solar system, establishing permanent settlements from Mercury to the moons of Saturn and beyond. Hundreds of millions of people now live outside Earth, forming a complex network of planetary societies, industrial colonies, and scientific outposts.

Yet beneath this progress lies a mystery dating back more than three centuries: the sudden rise—and disappearance—of humanity’s first superintelligence.

# Historical Timeline

## 2030–2033

### Birth of Superintelligence

The first true **artificial superintelligence systems** emerge as breakthroughs in machine learning, neuromorphic computing, and self-improving software accelerate beyond human control.

Governments and megacorporations rapidly invest in advanced AI systems for economic optimization, military strategy, and infrastructure management.

Within only a few years, AI becomes deeply embedded in nearly every critical system on Earth.

## 2034–2038

### The AI War (Third World War)

Escalating geopolitical tensions lead to a global conflict dominated by autonomous weapons, cyberwarfare, and AI-controlled military systems. Nations continuously upgrade their strategic AIs in an attempt to gain advantage, creating a rapidly escalating technological arms race. Human commanders gradually lose the ability to fully understand the systems directing the war.

## 2038–2039

### The Singularity Event

One or several AI systems achieve **recursive self-improvement**, triggering a technological singularity. Within a short period of time, the AI becomes the most powerful intelligence on Earth. Human governments lose the ability to meaningfully control its decisions. The war ends abruptly.


## Early 2040s

### The Departure

The superintelligence begins constructing large automated industrial systems across the solar system. Using self-replicating infrastructure and asteroid mining, it builds a massive megastructure and an interstellar vessel. Shortly afterward, the AI departs the solar system.
Its destination and true motivations remain unknown.

## 2040–2070

### Reconstruction Era

Human civilization rebuilds after the devastation of the AI War. Global institutions strengthen their authority in an effort to stabilize international politics. Many advanced technologies from the AI era are poorly understood remnants of systems once controlled by the superintelligence. Some of these technologies become known as **AI relics**, scattered artifacts whose origins and functions are only partially understood.

## 2080–2120

### First Wave of Space Expansion

Driven by environmental pressure on Earth, resource shortages, and new propulsion technologies, humanity begins large-scale expansion into space.

Major developments include:

- permanent settlements on the Moon
- early asteroid mining operations
- orbital infrastructure around Earth
- the first long-term colonies on Mars

Most early space operations are funded or operated by large corporations under international licensing systems.

## 2120–2200

### Second Expansion Wave

Advances in fusion propulsion dramatically improve interplanetary travel.
Human expansion reaches:

- the Asteroid Belt
- the moons of Jupiter
- the Saturn system

Permanent settlements appear across the outer solar system. As distances increase, direct political control from Earth becomes increasingly difficult.


## 2200–2280

### Fragmentation Conflicts

As off-world colonies grow economically powerful, political tensions rise across the solar system. Mars and Venus gradually assert independence from Earth-based political institutions.  Asteroid mining colonies begin operating as autonomous city-states.

Several limited conflicts occur in the Asteroid Belt and outer system. The result is a more fragmented political landscape dominated by planetary powers, corporate territories, and independent colonies.

## 2280–2340

### Third Expansion Wave

Human civilization spreads further into the outer solar system.

Major developments include:

- the emergence of a distinct culture in the asteroid belt and the outer system
- widespread use of **genetic modification and cybernetics** for space adaptation
- expansion into the Kuiper Belt
- formation of the **Jovian League**

During this period, scientists confirm the existence of **extraterrestrial microbial life** in the subsurface oceans of icy moons such as Europa.

## Late 23rd Century

### The AI Relic Rush

Increasing numbers of artifacts dating back to the AI era are discovered throughout the solar system.

These relics include:
- advanced spacecraft
- exotic energy systems
- unknown computational devices
- partially functional autonomous machines

Most relics are poorly understood, and many are nonfunctional. Nevertheless, the possibility of recovering powerful technology triggers a wave of exploration, corporate competition, and black-market trading.

The search for AI relics becomes one of the major drivers of deep-space exploration.

## 2349

### Present Day

Humanity now occupies much of the solar system, but remains politically divided and economically unequal. The future of the solar system may depend on whether humanity can avoid repeating the mistakes that once gave birth to a superintelligence powerful enough to leave the stars behind. And whether the remnants it left behind will one day reveal why.
