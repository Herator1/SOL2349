# ⚡ Reflex Booster

**Cat:** Combat | **Type:** Neural | **mSpace:** 3

**Flavor:** You act faster than everyone else.

**Overdrive Trigger:** +1 Action (this turn), that Action gains **Tactical Edge** → **+2 Load**

**Reflex Spike (Aug):** +1 Action (turn or reactive), gains **Tactical Edge**, ignores movement penalties → **+2 Load**

**Neural Correction:** After failed combat roll → **reroll** → **+3 Load**  
_Limit:_ 1× per round

# 🎯 Targeting Suite

**Cat:** Combat | **Type:** Neural / Optic | **mSpace:** 2

**Flavor:** You predict movement before it happens.

**Predictive Targeting:** Tactical Edge on attacks; ignore movement penalties; reduce cover bonus by up to −2 → **+1 Load**

**Recoil Dampening (Passive):** first repetition penalty ignored (2nd attack = no penalty)

**Lock-On (Aug):** mark target → all attacks this turn gain **+2 to hit** → **+2 Load**

---

# 🔪 Close Quarters Suite

**Cat:** Combat + Physical | **Type:** Muscular / Neural | **mSpace:** 3

**Flavor:** Distance is something you remove.

**Bull Rush (Aug):** move 10 m toward target (turn or reactive), ignore terrain & penalties → **+2 Load**

**Impact Strike (Optional):** after Bull Rush → 1 melee attack with **Tactical Edge**, counts toward repetition → **+3 Load (total)**

_Limits:_ 1× per turn; does not interrupt triggering action

---

**Enhanced Strikes (Passive):** melee attacks → **+1 damage die**

**Close Combat Advantage (Passive):** Tactical Edge on melee & grappling

**Augmented Throw (Aug):** throw melee weapon (uses melee skill), +1 damage die, ignore short-range penalties → **+1 Load**

**Relentless Assault (Passive):** first melee repetition penalty ignored
