## 🗨️ Persuasion Suite

**Cat:** Social  
**Type:** Neural / Behavioral  
**mSpace:** 2

A behavioral influence implant built to guide decisions, identify leverage, and push conversations toward useful outcomes.

### Leverage Point
**Type:** Passive

You can quickly identify one likely emotional or practical leverage point in a conversation, such as:
- fear
- desire
- status concern
- insecurity
- urgency
- personal motive

The GM should provide one usable angle when enough information is present.

### Directed Argument
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 10 m  
**Load:** +1

Choose one visible target within range.

Your **next social interaction** against that target gains **Tactical Edge**.

### Pressure Shift
**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +2

Choose one visible target within range.

Until the start of your next turn, that target suffers **Setback** on attempts to resist persuasion, negotiation, or command pressure from you.

**Role:** negotiation / influence

## 🎭 Deception Module

**Cat:** Social  
**Type:** Neural / Cognitive  
**mSpace:** 2

A deception implant built for fast lies, plausible cover, and controlled misdirection under pressure.

### Cover Story
**Type:** Passive

You can rapidly construct a plausible false identity, explanation, or surface-level story that fits the immediate situation.

This does not guarantee success, but it gives you a coherent false narrative instantly.

### False Signal
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 10 m  
**Load:** +1

Choose one visible target within range.

Deliver a short, plausible false statement or misleading cue.

If the lie is believable within the situation, the target treats it as true long enough for one immediate reaction, hesitation, or decision.

### Misdirection
**Type:** Augmented  
**Load:** +2

Until the start of your next turn, attempts to detect your deception, concealment, or false intent suffer **Setback**.

**Role:** infiltration / deception

## 🧭 Command Uplink

**Cat:** Social  
**Type:** Neural / Coordination  
**mSpace:** 2

A coordination implant built to impose structure, timing, and decisive team action under pressure.

### Tactical Order
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 10 m  
**Load:** +1

Choose one ally within range.

That ally gains **Tactical Edge** on their next action.

### Action Transfer
**Type:** Active  
**Cost:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 10 m  
**Load:** +3  
**Limit:** 1× per round

Choose one ally within range.

That ally gains **+1 Action** this turn.

This does not stack with another **Command Uplink**.

### Battlefield Structure
**Type:** Passive

Gain **Tactical Edge** on **Command** when directing allies under pressure, confusion, or active danger.

**Role:** team coordination
