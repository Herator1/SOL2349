# 🧰 **Tools, Devices & Equipment**

Tools and devices extend a character’s ability to interact with the world.  
They do not replace skills—but they often determine **whether an action is possible at all**.

---

## ⚙️ **Usage Rules**

- Most tools require **1 Action** to use
- There are **no bonus actions**
- Some tools require **specific skills** to operate effectively

---

👉 Using a tool always competes with:

- attacking
- moving
- using implants

---

## 🧠 **Tool Traits**

|Trait|Effect|
|---|---|
|**Sensor**|Grants Tactical Edge on detection or analysis tasks|
|**Digital**|Required for hacking or system interaction|
|**Engineering**|Required for repair and mechanical actions|
|**Medical**|Required for treatment and diagnostics|
|**Industrial**|Can affect structures and heavy materials|
|**Explosive**|Causes structural damage or forced entry|
|**EVA**|Required for safe operation in vacuum|
|**Encrypted**|Cannot be intercepted without advanced systems|
|**Utility**|General-purpose tool, broad but limited effectiveness|

# ⚠️ **Special Rules**

---

## 🔓 **Tool Requirements**

Some actions cannot be performed without the correct tool:

- hacking → requires **Digital tool**
- advanced repairs → require **Engineering tools**
- medical treatment → requires **Medical tools**

---

👉 Without the proper tool:

- action may be impossible
- or suffer **Complication**

---

## 🧠 **Tool Quality & Impact**

Tools can:

- enable an action
- reduce difficulty
- grant **Tactical Edge**

---

## ⚙️ **Industrial Tools as Weapons**

Industrial tools can be used as improvised weapons:

- use appropriate skill (usually Coordination or Fitness)
- may gain **special traits** (e.g. Energy, Ignore Armor)
- often require close range

---

## 🌌 **EVA Dependency**

In vacuum:

- EVA gear is mandatory
- missing equipment may cause:
    - Complication
    - direct damage or Wounds
