Time during combat and other high-intensity situations is measured in **rounds**. One round represents roughly **five seconds** in the game world, during which all characters and NPCs take their turns.

On their turn, a character has **two actions**, which can be used for activities such as **moving (up to 10 meters), attacking, operating equipment, or interacting with objects**. Characters may also take **one bonus action**, representing quick or minor activities such as shouting a warning, activating a simple ability, or briefly focusing.

In addition, each character has **one reaction per round**, which can be used when specific situations occur outside their turn, such as responding to danger, taking cover, or triggering certain abilities.
