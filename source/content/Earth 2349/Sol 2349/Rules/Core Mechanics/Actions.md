Time during combat and other high-intensity situations is measured in **rounds**. One round represents roughly **five seconds** in the game world, during which all characters and NPCs take their turns.

On their turn, a character has **two actions**, which can be used for activities such as **moving (up to 10 meters), attacking, operating equipment, or interacting with objects**. Characters may also take **one bonus action**, representing quick or minor activities such as shouting a warning, activating a simple ability, or briefly focusing.

In addition, each character has **one reaction per round**, which can be used when specific situations occur outside their turn, such as responding to danger, taking cover, or triggering certain abilities.

Actions can be used **multiple times per turn** (e.g. attacking twice), but repeated use of the same action may incur **cumulative penalties**.

---

# Standard Actions

| Action                    | Cost                | Effect                                                                                                                                                   |
| ------------------------- | ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Move**                  | 1 Action            | Move up to 10 meters. Difficult terrain or obstacles may reduce distance.                                                                                |
| **Attack (Melee/Ranged)** | 1 Action            | Make an attack roll. Consecutive attacks in the same turn suffer **−4 cumulative penalty per additional attack**.                                        |
| **Take Aim**              | 1 Action            | Gain **+4 to the next attack**. That attack also crits on **19–20**. Ends after the next attack.                                                         |
| **Throw**                 | 1 Action            | Throw an object (grenade, tool, etc.). Uses **Melee skill** for accuracy.                                                                                |
| **Take Cover**            | 1 Action            | Restore Guard (**+2**, or **+3 in solid/full cover**). Cannot exceed max Guard. Consecutive uses: **−1 effectiveness per additional use**.               |
| **Overwatch**             | 1 Action + Reaction | End your turn and set a trigger (e.g. “enemy leaves cover”). When triggered, make an attack using your reaction. Can be combined with **Take Aim**.      |
| **Suppressive Fire**      | 1 Action + Reaction | Ranged attack at **−4 to hit**. If the target **attacks or leaves cover**, immediately make a second attack (−4) as a reaction. Firearm only. Ends turn. |
| **Interact**              | 1 Action            | Operate devices, open doors, reload, activate systems, use equipment.                                                                                    |
| **Use Implant / Ability** | Usually 1 Action    | Activate implants or special abilities. Specific effects depend on the system used.                                                                      |
| **Reload**                | 1 Action            | Clear the low ammo condition.                                                                                                                            |

# Bonus Actions

|Action|Cost|Effect|
|---|---|---|
|**Shout / Communicate**|Bonus|Short verbal communication.|
|**Focus Briefly**|Bonus|Minor mental reset or preparation (GM may grant small situational bonus).|
|**Quick Scan / Look**|Bonus|Observe a target, object, or area quickly.|
|**Command**|Bonus|Issue a short tactical order (may interact with abilities or team mechanics).|
|**Implant (Quick)**|Bonus|Some implants allow activation as a bonus action.|

# Reactions

Reactions can only be used when a **trigger condition** occurs.

Common examples:

|Reaction|Trigger|Effect|
|---|---|---|
|**Opportunity Attack**|Enemy leaves melee range|Make a free melee attack|
|**Overwatch Trigger**|Defined condition|Perform stored attack|
|**Suppressive Trigger**|Target moves or attacks|Immediate follow-up attack|
|**Ability / Implant**|Varies|Depends on system|
Each character has **one reaction per round**.
