_SOL 2349_ is designed as a **hard science fiction roleplaying game grounded in plausible technology and believable human limits**. The system aims to capture the tension and danger of life in space while remaining **fast and intuitive at the table**. While the setting draws heavily on real physics, engineering and human physiology, the rules deliberately avoid becoming a full simulation. Whenever realism and playability conflict, **clarity and flow take priority**.

The mechanics are built around a **simple core resolution system using a d20**, allowing players to resolve most actions quickly while still supporting meaningful character specialization and progression. The goal is a system that feels **credible and immersive**, where equipment, environment and human capability matter, but where players can focus on **decisions, teamwork and storytelling rather than complex calculations**.
