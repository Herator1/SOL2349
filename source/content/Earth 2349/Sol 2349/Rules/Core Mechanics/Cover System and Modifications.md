# 🎯 **Defense, Cover & Positioning**

In _SOL 2349_, survival in combat depends heavily on **cover, positioning, and angles of attack**.

There is **no passive defense**.  
A character is hard to hit because of **where they are, how they move, and how they react**.

---
## 🧱 **Cover**

Cover increases the **Difficulty (DC)** of attacks against a target.

|Cover Type|DC Modifier|Description|
|---|---|---|
|**No Cover**|+0|Fully exposed|
|**Light Cover**|+2|Partial obstruction (railings, crates)|
|**Medium Cover**|+4|Solid protection (corners, machinery)|
|**Full Cover**|—|Cannot be targeted directly|

> Full cover blocks attacks completely unless the attacker changes position.

---
## 🧍 **Positioning**

A character’s stance affects how easy they are to hit.

|Position|Effect|
|---|---|
|**Standing**|no modifier|
|**Crouched**|+1 DC vs ranged attacks|
|**Prone**|+2 DC vs ranged attacks, −2 DC vs melee attacks|
Changing position is usually part of movement or requires **1 Action** (GM discretion).

---

## 🔄 **Movement & Targeting**

Movement directly affects how easy a character is to hit.

|Target State|Effect|
|---|---|
|**Stationary**|no modifier|
|**Moving**|+1 DC vs ranged attacks|
|**Fast movement / sprint**|+2 DC vs ranged attacks|
|**Erratic / zero-g drift**|+2 DC and may impose Complication|

👉 These modifiers apply **only while the target is actively moving**

---

## 🔁 **Reactive Defense**

Characters can respond to attacks using **Reactive Actions**.

### Examples:

- diving behind cover
- sudden movement
- evasive maneuvers

---
## 🔁 **Evade**

A character can attempt to evade an incoming attack.

---
### ⏱️ **Timing**

Evade must be declared:

👉 **after the attack is declared, but before the roll is made**

---
### ⚙️ **Effect**

- Spend **1 Action**
- Gain **+2 DC** against that attack
- Counts as **movement**

---
### 📍 **Position & Movement**

Evade represents a **sudden defensive movement**, not full repositioning.

- May allow **minor repositioning** (step, shift, lean)
- Does **not replace full movement**

---
## 🌌 **Special Situations**

### 🟢 **Prone**

- You can Evade
- Effect unchanged
- Narratively: rolling, shifting, flattening

---
### 🟢 **Zero-G / Free Drift**

- You can Evade
- BUT:

👉 GM may apply:

- **Complication**, or
- reduce effect to **+1 DC**

Reason: no stable surface → harder to push off

---
### 🟢 **Restricted Movement (tight space, restrained, heavy gear)**

- Evade may:
    - be reduced in effectiveness
    - or be disallowed (GM discretion)

---

## 🔁 **Interaction with Other Modifiers**

Evade stacks with:

- cover
- positioning
- movement

👉 but still follows normal limits (GM sanity check)
---

## 📐 **Angles & Exposure**

Cover only works **from the direction it actually protects**.

If an attacker gains a better angle:

- moving around cover
- flanking
- attacking from elevation
- shooting from behind or above

→ the target may receive **reduced or no cover bonus**

---

## ⚖️ **Tactical Edge**

When a character has a clear tactical advantage:

👉 roll **2d20, take the better result**

---

### Examples:

- target is fully exposed
- superior angle (flank, elevation)
- target is distracted or suppressed
- attacker is concealed

---

## ⚠️ **Complication**

When conditions hinder a character:

👉 roll **2d20, take the worse result**

---

### Examples:

- low visibility (smoke, darkness)
- unstable footing or zero-g
- firing while moving fast
- sensor interference

---

## 🔁 **Reactive Attacks**

Attacks made as **Reactive Actions** are harder to execute.

👉 apply:

- **−2 to the attack roll**

If the attacker is also:

- moving → additional −1 or Complication
- under pressure (high Load, unstable position) → GM may apply Complication

---

## ⚙️ **Stacking Rules**

- **Cover** → modifies DC
- **Position & Movement** → modify DC
- **Edge / Complication** → modify the roll
- **Reactive Attacks** → apply penalties

All of these can be combined.

---

## 🧪 **Example**

Target behind medium cover, crouched, and moving:

Base DC = 10  
+4 (cover)  
+1 (crouched)  
+1 (movement)

👉 **Total DC = 16**

Attacker fires as a Reactive Action:

- −2 to attack roll

Attacker has Tactical Edge:

- roll 2d20 → take better
