In SOL 2349, survival in combat depends heavily on **cover, positioning, and angles of attack**. There is no passive defense—being hard to hit is the result of where you are and how you move.

## Cover

Cover increases the **Difficulty (DC)** of attacks against a target.

|Cover Type|DC Modifier|Description|
|---|---|---|
|**No Cover**|+0|Fully exposed|
|**Light Cover**|+2|Partial obstruction (railings, crates)|
|**Medium Cover**|+4|Solid protection (corners, machinery)|
|**Full Cover**|—|Cannot be targeted directly|
> Full cover blocks attacks completely unless the attacker changes position.

---
## Positioning

A character’s stance affects how easy they are to hit.

|Position|Effect|
|---|---|
|**Standing**|no modifier|
|**Crouched**|+1 DC vs ranged attacks|
|**Prone**|+2 DC vs ranged attacks, −2 DC vs melee attacks|

Changing position is usually part of movement or requires minimal effort (GM discretion).

---

## Angles & Exposure

Cover only works **from the direction it actually protects**.

If an attacker gains a better angle:

- moving around cover
    
- flanking
    
- attacking from elevation
    
- shooting from behind or above
    

→ the target may receive **reduced or no cover bonus**

---

## Tactical Edge 

When a character has a **clear tactical superiority**, they gain **Tactical Edge**:

Roll 2d20 → take the better result

---

### Examples of Tactical Edge

- target is fully exposed (no cover, unaware)
    
- attacker has superior angle (flank, elevation)
    
- target is distracted or suppressed
    
- target is in bright light while attacker is concealed
    

---

## Complication 

When conditions hinder a character, they suffer **Complications**:

Roll 2d20 → take the worse result

---

### Examples of Complication

- dim light
    
- smoke, dust, fog
    
- partial blindness or sensor interference
    
- firing while moving fast
    
- unstable position (e.g. zero-g drift)
    

---

## Stacking Rules

- **Cover modifies DC**
    
- **Position modifies DC**
    
- **Edge / Complication modifies the roll**
    

They can be combined.

---

### Example

Target behind medium cover and crouched:

Base DC 10  
+4 (cover)  
+1 (crouched)  
  
Total DC = 15

Attacker has Tactical Edge:

Roll 2d20 → take better  
+ modifiers vs DC 15
