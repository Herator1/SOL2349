When a character attempts an action with an uncertain outcome, the Game Master assigns a **Difficulty Class (DC)**. The player rolls **1d20 + relevant Attribute + Skill modifier**. If the result is **equal to or higher than the DC**, the action succeeds.

Typical modifier ranges for characters:

- **+2** – easily achievable with basic training
- **+4** – competent specialist
- **+6** – highly skilled 
- **+8** – elite professional 
- **+10** – exceptional expert

  ### Difficulty Examples

|Difficulty|DC|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Routine|8|Basic repair, simple negotiation|
|Standard|10|Typical professional task|
|Challenging|13|Difficult maneuver or complex system|
|Hard|16|Emergency repair, dangerous maneuver|
|Extreme|19|Only experts succeed reliably|
|Near Impossible|22+|Exceptional or experimental task|
