# 🧠 **Mental Strain & Psychological Pressure**

Life in space places constant psychological pressure on the human mind.

Isolation, confined environments, and extreme risk can push even experienced operators beyond their limits.

In _SOL 2349_, this is not tracked as a separate resource, but as **Mental Conditions**.

---

## ⚠️ **Mental Conditions**

Mental strain is represented in four escalating states:

|Level|Condition|Effect|
|---|---|---|
|1|**Unsettled**|−1 to concentration-based actions|
|2|**Shaken**|−2 to concentration-based actions|
|3|**Strained**|−2 to all actions|
|4|**Breaking Point**|Loss of control (GM determines outcome)|

---

## 🔁 **Gaining Mental Strain**

Mental Conditions can be caused by:

- Overload (implant stress)
- extreme danger (e.g. decompression, near death)
- psychological attacks
- witnessing trauma

---

## 💥 **Breaking Point**

At Level 4:

- the character temporarily loses control
- the GM determines the reaction, such as:
    - panic movement
    - freezing
    - withdrawal
    - irrational action

Afterwards:

- reduce Mental Condition by 1

---

## 🔗 **Interaction with Load**

Load and mental strain are closely linked:

- high Load may increase Mental Conditions
- Overload effects may trigger mental strain
- sustained pressure pushes characters toward breakdown

---
## 🔁 **Recovery**

Mental strain recovers gradually and requires **time, safety, or support**.

---

### 🕒 **Short Rest (10–15 minutes)**

- Reduce Mental Condition by **1 level**

Requirements:

- relative safety
- no immediate threat

---

### 🧠 **Psychological Support**

Psychological support can only be provided **during a Short Rest**.

- A character trained in **Psychology** may assist one ally
- successful test → reduce Mental Condition by **1 additional level**

---

👉 This represents:

- guided breathing
- grounding techniques
- cognitive stabilization

---

### ⚠️ **Limitations**

- Short Rests **cannot be taken consecutively**
- Mental recovery requires:
    - time under pressure
    - or meaningful activity

before it can occur again

---

### 🛌 **Extended Rest (6–8 hours)**

- Remove **all Mental Conditions**

Requires:

- safe environment
- proper rest and recovery

---

### ⚠️ **Under Pressure**

Mental Conditions do **not recover** when:

- in combat
- under active threat
- exposed to ongoing stress

(GM discretion)
