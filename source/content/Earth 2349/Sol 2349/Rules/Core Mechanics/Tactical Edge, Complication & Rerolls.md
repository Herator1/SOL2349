# 🎲 **Tactical Edge, Complication & Rerolls**

---

## ⚖️ **Tactical Edge & Complication**

Situational factors may influence a roll:

- **Tactical Edge** → roll **2d20**, keep the higher result
- **Complication** → roll **2d20**, keep the lower result

---

### Rules

- Only **one** of these can apply at a time
- If both apply → they cancel out
- The roll is always resolved as **a single final result**

---

## 🔁 **Rerolls**

Some abilities, implants, or effects allow a **reroll**.

---

### Core Rule

A reroll:

- replaces the original result completely
- must be used, even if worse
- can only be used **once per test**

---

### ⏱️ **Timing**

A reroll must be declared:

👉 **after the roll is made, but before the outcome is resolved**

---

## 🔗 **Interaction with Edge & Complication**

This is the important part:

---

### If using Tactical Edge or Complication:

- You reroll **the entire roll**
- (i.e. both dice)

👉 Then apply Edge / Complication again

---

### Example:

- You have Tactical Edge
- Roll: 2d20 → pick highest
- You reroll → roll 2d20 again → pick highest

---

## ⚠️ **Limitations**

- No stacking rerolls
- No rerolling a reroll
- Rerolls do not remove:
    - penalties
    - Load effects
    - Complications

---

# 🧬 **Rerolls & Implants**

Rerolls are primarily granted by **Implants and abilities**.

---

## Design Space

Implants may:

- allow a reroll
- allow rerolls under conditions
- improve rerolls

---

### Examples

---

### **Neural Correction Module**

- Once per round:  
    → reroll a failed test

---

### **Predictive Cortex**

- Reroll allowed
- If both rolls fail → take lesser penalty

---

### **Overclocked Analysis (Risky)**

- You may reroll
- Gain **+1 Load**
