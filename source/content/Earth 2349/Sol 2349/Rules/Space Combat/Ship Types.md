# Ship Types (Reference Classes)

This section provides **baseline ship classes** used as reference templates for Narrative Space Combat.

These are not specific ship models but **generic performance profiles** that can be used to quickly create ships or to benchmark custom designs.

Each class lists:

- ship statistics
    
- number of hardpoints
    
- typical weapon configuration
    

GMs and players may freely modify these templates.

---

# Civilian Ships

Most civilian ships are **not designed for combat**.  
They usually rely on **speed, escorts, or diplomacy** rather than heavy weapons.

Civilian ships rarely mount offensive weapons and often only carry **basic point defense systems**.

# Battleship

Battleships are the largest and most heavily armed warships commonly encountered in the Solar System.  
They are extremely rare due to their enormous cost, logistical demands, and political implications.

Most factions maintain **only a handful of battleships**, if any. These vessels are usually assigned to guard **critical strategic infrastructure**, such as:

- major orbital shipyards
    
- planetary defense zones
    
- key trade hubs
    
- high-value research installations
    

Because of their overwhelming firepower and defensive systems, battleships are rarely deployed alone and often operate as **command vessels for larger fleets**.

Player characters will almost never command a battleship. In most campaigns they serve as **strategic background elements** or major plot devices.

# Typical Battleship Loadout

Battleships mount a wide variety of heavy weapon systems and defensive arrays.

Typical configuration:

- 2 Torpedo Launchers
    
- 2 Missile Racks
    
- 2 Heavy Railguns
    
- 1 Laser Array
    
- 5 Point Defense Batteries
    

These ships rely on **long-range missile and torpedo strikes** supported by powerful direct-fire weapons once enemies close distance.

Their extensive defensive systems make them extremely difficult to overwhelm with missile attacks.

---

# Combat Role

Battleships dominate large-scale engagements and can control huge areas of space.

Typical roles include:

- orbital defense platforms
    
- fleet command ships
    
- strategic deterrence
    

A single battleship can outmatch most smaller vessels, but they are rarely risked in uncertain engagements due to their strategic importance.
