## Overview

Vesta occupies a critical position within the Asteroid Belt, both geographically and strategically. Its relatively stable structure and favorable location have made it a natural hub for **traffic control and security operations**.

Unlike many other Belt settlements, Vesta is highly structured. Infrastructure is planned, maintained, and actively controlled. The asteroid hosts patrol fleets, escort services, and monitoring systems designed to secure major transport routes.

This makes Vesta one of the few places in the Belt where authority is clearly defined—and enforced.

For traders and civilian traffic, Vesta represents stability.  
For pirates and independent operators, it represents constraint.
