## Ishtar Gate
![[Ishtar gate.png|450]]
**Population:** ~65 million

Located above the highlands of **Ishtar Terra**, Ishtar Gate is the primary **industrial and manufacturing center** of Venus. The city is dominated by vast fabrication complexes, polymer refineries and industrial megaplatforms that produce structural materials for orbital habitats and spacecraft across the inner solar system. Much of the heavy industrial workforce of Venus lives here.
