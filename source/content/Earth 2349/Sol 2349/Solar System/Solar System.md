In **SOL 2349**, the Solar System has become humanity’s extended home. Over the past centuries, permanent settlements, industrial infrastructure, and research installations have spread from Earth to the outer reaches of the inner system. Planets, moons, asteroids, and orbital habitats are now connected by a dense network of transport routes, energy systems, and corporate-controlled facilities.

Human presence varies widely across the system. While [[Earth]] remains the largest population center and [[Mars]] has grown into a major scientific and agricultural hub, much of the Solar System is dominated by **industrial stations, mining outposts, and research installations** operating in extreme environments. Vast orbital structures harvest energy, refine raw materials, and support the logistics required to maintain interplanetary civilization.

This section provides an overview of the **major locations of the Solar System in 2349**, including inhabited planets, orbital stations, industrial megastructures, and remote research facilities that shape the political and economic landscape of humanity beyond Earth.
