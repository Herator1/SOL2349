**SOL 2349** is a tabletop roleplaying game set in a realistic vision of the Solar System three centuries in the future. Humanity has spread across planets, moons, and orbital habitats, but space remains a harsh and unforgiving frontier shaped by physics, economics, and human ambition.

The game focuses on **hard science fiction**, emphasizing believable technology, realistic space travel, and a living interplanetary economy dominated by powerful megacorporations. There are no aliens, no superintelligent AI—only humans pushing the limits of survival and expansion in space.

SOL 2349 combines **simple and fast rules** with **deep character development**. Players can take on the roles of freighter crews, corporate agents, engineers, explorers, scientists, or mercenaries navigating the political, economic, and personal conflicts of a growing civilization in space.

With a setting spanning the entire [[Solar System]]—from the crowded orbital cities of [[Earth]] to the industrial mines of the Asteroid Belt and the research arcologies of Mars—SOL 2349 offers **endless possibilities for stories** of exploration, survival, corporate intrigue, and human resilience beyond Earth.
