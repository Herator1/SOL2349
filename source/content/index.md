# SOL 2349 – A Hard Science Fiction Roleplaying Game

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/yKQ1wCXSMxo" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

**SOL 2349** is a tabletop roleplaying game and worldbuilding project set in a realistic vision of the Solar System three centuries in the future.

Humanity has spread across planets, moons, and orbital habitats, but space remains harsh, expensive, and unforgiving. The setting focuses on **hard science fiction**: believable technology, realistic space travel, and an interplanetary civilization shaped by physics, economics, and human ambition. There are no aliens, no miracle technology, and no benevolent superintelligent AI—only humans pushing the limits of survival and expansion in space.

Players can take on the roles of freighter crews, corporate agents, engineers, explorers, scientists, or mercenaries navigating the political, economic, and personal conflicts of life beyond Earth.

With a setting spanning the entire [[Solar System]]—from the crowded orbital infrastructure of [[Earth]] to the industrial mines of the [[Asteroid Belt]] and the research arcologies of [[Mars]]—SOL 2349 offers stories of exploration, survival, corporate intrigue, and human resilience under pressure.
